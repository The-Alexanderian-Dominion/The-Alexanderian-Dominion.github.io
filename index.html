What is The Alexanderian Dominion?

When Alexander The Great took over the country of Egypt, he founded a new city known as Alexanderia and this city is famous for hosting wonders of the world such as the library of Alexanderia. The Alexanderian Dominion is a Micronation with the goal of restoring the library of Alexanderia.

Our nation, is going to host as much as human knowledge possible, with the help of modern tools such as web scrapers, aritificial intelligence, etc. Basically, we're considering ourselves a Cyber Nation where people can join and start communicating around the topcis such as knowledge, science and philosophy.

Use of AI

Since this nation has been founded by Muhammadreza Haghiri, Iranian entrepreneur and AI expert, one of the key elements of collecting human knowledge is using artificial intelligence. Since most of these Large Language Models are trained on the data from Wikipedia, different websites and scientific articles, it can be a good source of generating new knowledge and keep it in the library of Alexanderia.

AI is also used for governing and decision making in The Alexanderian Dominion as well. Models can explore historic documents and events and provide consulting to our politicians and diplomats.
